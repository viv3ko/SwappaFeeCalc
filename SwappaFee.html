FEE CALCULATOR

INFORMATION

Function: This calculator calculates the Buyers Fee, the Sellers Fee, Paypals Deduction, Listing Price and Sellers Gain of a potential device sale based of a specific input Asking Price.

Instructions: Input the Asking Price (in dollars) you wish to sell your device for. The application shall calculate the desired values.

Disclaimer: All values are approximations as I cannot take in account for the rounding that Swappa uses.
TERMINOLOGY

Ask Price: The dollar amount you want from the sale.

Sellers Fee: The amount you owe Swappa (deducted from your Ask Price).

Buyers Fee: The amount the buyer owes Swappa (added to your original Ask Price).

PayPal Deduction: The amount you owe PayPal (deducted from your Ask Price).

Listing Price: The price a potential buyer will see (Ask Price + Buyers Fee).

Payout: Approximate payout made from the sale (what you should see in PayPal).
